About BGIET

Bhai Gurdas Institute of Engineering and Technology (BGIET), Sangrur, is one of Punjab’s most respected educational institutions. Established with a vision of excellence in engineering and management education, BGIET provides a vibrant environment for innovation, skill development, and academic growth. The institute boasts modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on industry-oriented learning, ensuring students are career-ready and future-focused.

Our Culture

BGIET is a melting pot of diversity, where students from various states of India and other countries, including African nations, come together to create a vibrant community. The college provides a comfortable and well-equipped hostel for students, fostering a sense of belonging. Physical games and sports facilities are available to keep students active and engaged, while a modern gym supports their fitness goals. The college library offers a vast collection of resources to aid academic excellence. BGIET organizes events from time to time, such as cultural fests and technical symposiums, for the amusement and growth of students. College trips are arranged to explore new places and build camaraderie, while study remains the top priority. Students are frequently taken on industrial visits to gain real-world exposure, ensuring they are well-prepared for their careers.

Placements

BGIET has an excellent placement record, with top companies visiting our campus every year. Our dedicated Training and Placement Cell ensures students are well-prepared for interviews and industry challenges through workshops, mock interviews, and skill development programs. Companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and many more have recruited our graduates, offering promising career opportunities.

Our Faculty

Our faculty at BGIET comprises experienced educators and industry experts who are committed to shaping the future of our students. With a blend of academic excellence and practical knowledge, they provide personalized guidance and foster critical thinking. Their dedication ensures that students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for real-world challenges.
